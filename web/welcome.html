Our Awesome Features

Child-Friendly Drawing Interface

The Magic Sketchpad's intuitive and child-friendly drawing interface ensures that even the youngest artists can jump right into the world of creativity. With easy-to-use controls and a vibrant palette of colors, children can bring their imaginations to life with a simple touch.

AI-Powered Prediction

SketchY lies in its AI capabilities. When children draw, the AI model kicks into action, attempting to predict the object or concept they're drawing. This instant feedback adds an element of surprise and excitement to their artistic journey

Drawing Completion Assistance

AI model can provide suggestions to complete their drawings. This collaborative approach encourages children to experiment and learn different artistic techniques.

Wide Range of Categories

With an impressive array of 16 categories, children can explore drawing a diverse range of subjects, from animals and nature to everyday objects. This expansive selection encourages exploration and helps children broaden their creative horizons..

Interactive Feedback

Each stroke on Sketchpad is met with instant feedback, creating a dynamic learning loop. Children can observe how their drawings align with the AI model's predictions, enhancing their understanding of shapes, lines, and artistic representation.

Responsive and Safe Environment

The Magic Sketchpad provides a responsive design that adapts seamlessly to various devices, ensuring accessibility for children across desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. With a focus on child safety, the app offers a secure online environment.
